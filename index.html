Why Cape Coral?

Cape Coral is one of Florida's best-kept secrets — a city laced with over 400 miles of navigable canals (more than Venice, Italy!) and blessed with warm Gulf breezes year-round.

Whether you're here to fish, kayak, explore Sanibel Island, or simply sip a cocktail on a canal dock at golden hour, Cape Coral delivers the quintessential Florida experience.

  • World-class fishing & boating
  • Minutes from Gulf beaches
  • Vibrant dining & nightlife
  • Sanibel & Fort Myers nearby
  • Manatees & dolphin watching
  • Year-round warm weather
